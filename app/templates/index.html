Evidence Surveillance Synthesis and Sharing (3S3)

A shared space for humans and machines to work together to update systematic reviews.

The platform was designed to provide an open and accessible list of connections between systematic reviews and clinical trials, keep track of new clinical trials that are relevant to already-published systematic reviews, and check whether a new systematic review might be needed for a specific clinical question.

It is also a work-in-progress and many of the features are currently only available to registered users. Visit our information page to learn more.
