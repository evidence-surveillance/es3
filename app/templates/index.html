trial2rev

a shared space for humans and machines to work together to update systematic reviews.

The trial2rev system is designed to provide an open and accessible list of connections between systematic reviews and clinical trials, keep track of new clinical trials that are relevant to already-published systematic reviews, and quickly estimate whether a published systematic review might be in need of updating.

It is also a work-in-progress and many of the features are currently only available to registered users. For more information on usage, how to contact us, and the best ways to credit us when you use the system in your research, visit “how to use” and “more information”.
