download data

If you are interested in the data collected by our system, you may download our entire set of systematic reviews and their respective verified*, included and relevant trials, along with the net number of votes for each trial-review link, here.

You may also download the subset of reviews that have complete a list of verified trials here. Note that this file only contains the verified trials for each review, and will not include any relevant or included trials.

Both files are in the following format, with one row per review-trial link:
review PMID, NCT ID, net votes, relationship

You might find it useful to use the APIs provided by ClinicalTrials.gov and PubMed in conjunction with the PubMed IDs and NCT IDs in our data to resolve review and trial metadata.

If there's any other data within our system that you think could be useful to you, please don't hesitate to get in touch and we can try to accomodate you.

*verified trials are trials that have been verified by a human user to be included in the relevant systematic review
